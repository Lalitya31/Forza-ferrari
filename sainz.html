Carlos Sainz

Carlos Sainz Jr., born September 1, 1994, in Madrid, Spain, is the son of legendary rally champion Carlos Sainz Sr. He began his F1 journey with Toro Rosso in 2015 and later drove for Renault and McLaren. In 2021, he joined Ferrari, quickly building a reputation for consistency, teamwork, and racecraft. In his debut season with the Scuderia, Sainz outscored his teammate Leclerc and achieved multiple podiums. His calm demeanor and engineering insight make him a valuable asset to Ferrari’s development.
