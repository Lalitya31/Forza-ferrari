Guanyu Zhou

Guanyu Zhou, born May 30, 1999, in Shanghai, China, became the first full-time Chinese driver in Formula 1 when he joined Alfa Romeo in 2022. Although not a Ferrari factory driver, Zhou races with a Ferrari-powered team, making him a significant figure in the Ferrari engine family. He is known for his smooth driving style, consistent performances, and role as a trailblazer for motorsport in Asia. Zhou’s journey to F1 included strong results in Formula 2 and roles as a test and development driver for Alpine. His presence has helped expand F1’s global reach and inspires a new generation of fans in China.Zhou is China’s first Formula 1 driver, known for his calm composure and rapid development. Though not a Ferrari regular, he represents the growing global diversity in Formula 1.
