Charles Leclerc

Charles Leclerc, born on October 16, 1997, in Monaco, is one of the most promising young talents in Formula 1. He made his F1 debut with Sauber in 2018 before moving to Ferrari in 2019. In only his second season, he won two Grand Prix races — in Belgium and Italy — instantly becoming a Ferrari hero. Leclerc is known for his aggressive qualifying pace, strategic intelligence, and calm under pressure. As Ferrari’s lead driver in the post-Vettel era, he symbolizes the team's future hopes of championship glory.
